Utmost Wealth International

This research was undertaken to focus on the Adviser Payments section of the Utmost Wealth logged in website. The goals for the research involved finding out the user needs, how they used the site, what was important to them and what needs where not being met, this included the pain points, where the users found the site confusing and if any workarounds where being used.

The purpose of the research was to increase Adviser self-service in generating commission statements / payment statements.

The research was focused on internal staff users, and required 20 participants in the user group.

The participants of the internal staff user group were interviewed individually, a set of tasks were drawn up which the User Experience group observed and recorded how these tasks were performed. A record of the outcome was obtained by shadowing the participants and taking notes of any observations.
